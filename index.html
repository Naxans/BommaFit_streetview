BommaFit Streetview

Virtually ride indoors with Google Street View and bluetooth bike speed or cadence sensor.
If you use a speed bluetooth low energy sensor, the bicycle speed determines the power.
If you use a cadence sensor, the power is fixed at 50 watts. A cadence ble sensor is ideal
for seniors with a home trainer where speed is not important and usually mounting the
sensor can only be done on the crank of the pedal.
